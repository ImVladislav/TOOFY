1

CREATE WALLET

Download MetaMask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

2

GET SOME ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $TOOFY. If you don't have any ETH, you can buy directly on MetaMask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

3

GO TO UNISWAP

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your MetaMask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $TOOFY token address into Uniswap, select $TOOFY, and confirm.
